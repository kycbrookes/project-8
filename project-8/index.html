I'm a producer & DJ based in Cambridge, MA.

In high school I began collecting records, producing music with Ableton Live, and attending shows in the Boston area. Since then I have released original tracks and remixes, collaborated with friends online and in person, and DJed a handful of events.

I strongly believe in the power of the the Internet as a platform for connecting people, especially creatives. Most of the music I enjoy listening to is produced by young artists and shared on SoundCloud, a site I've come to rely on to post my own work, get feedback from my peers, and improve my craft.

As I set my sights on Los Angeles this fall, I will continue to grow as a producer by playing live sets, collaborating with others, and putting the music first... always.
